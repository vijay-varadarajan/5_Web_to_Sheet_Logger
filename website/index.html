How to Use

1. Install the Extension

Get Web to Sheet Logger from the Chrome Web Store and add it to your browser in seconds.

2. Sign in with Google

Securely connect your Google account to enable seamless access to your Sheets.

3. Link Your Spreadsheet

Choose or create a Google Sheet where your web data will be logged automatically.

4. Select Data on Any Webpage

Highlight or pick the information you want to capture directly from the web page.

5. Add tags to your highlighted text

Before saving, add one or more tags to your highlighted text to help you organize your data later.

6. Review & Analyze

Open your Google Sheet anytime to view, filter, and analyze your collected data.
